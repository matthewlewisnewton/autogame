**Design decisions** (documented in the tickets, all top-level ACs covered):
- **Zone placement:** fixed at the cast point (not following the player), mirroring `barrier_dome`'s `barrierDomeX/Z` pattern.
- **Duration / cooldown:** 2s zone (`durationMs` 3000→2000), ~8s cooldown (`cooldownMs` 18000→8000).
- **Rule (chose one):** *targeting suppression* — enemies cannot detect/target a player standing in the fog, and in-progress wind-ups against a now-smoked player miss. This is deterministic and directly testable (the "targeting cleared while in zone" AC option), unlike a probabilistic miss-rate.
- **Split rationale:** 01 establishes cast + zone state + cooldown + allow-list; 02 is the gameplay effect with tests; 03 is the client VFX.

All three are **`code`** verification: the smoke effect lives in server state/logic and the VFX only fires when the item is used (which the visual happy-path run never does), so screenshots couldn't confirm any of it.
