**Rationale for the split:**
- **01** is purely additive infra (new `models.js` with cached `loadModel` + all-`null` `MODEL_REGISTRY`, `publicDir` in vite, committed empty `public/models/.gitkeep`) and touches zero rendering code — so it carries no risk of altering visuals.
- **02** wires the registry into the four hot render paths (`createPlayerAvatar`, `createEnemyMesh`, `createMinionMesh`, `createLootMesh`) with synchronous procedural fallback, the only place pixel-identical behavior could regress.

Keeping these in separate passes isolates the risky render-path edit from the foundation, and each is independently checkable from the diff + logs.
