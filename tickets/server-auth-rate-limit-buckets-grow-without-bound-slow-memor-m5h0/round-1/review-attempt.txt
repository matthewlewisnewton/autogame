### Blocking gap

`startRateLimitSweep()` is called in `startServer()` **before** `clearAllTimers()`, which immediately calls `stopRateLimitSweep()`. `restartBackgroundTimers()` does not restart the auth sweep, so the periodic prune **never runs on a live server**. The original unbounded Map growth from expired, never-revisited keys is still present in production.

**Fix:** Move `startRateLimitSweep()` to after `clearAllTimers()` / `restartBackgroundTimers()` in `game/server/index.js`.
