## Polish Field Medic Lock-On Stat Labels

The Field Medic lock-on panel currently shows medic-specific surfaced stats with raw camelCase labels such as `healAmount`, `healCooldownMs`, and `fleeSpeed`. The information is present and functional, but player-facing labels would be clearer if formatted like the existing common stats.

### Acceptance Criteria
- The Field Medic lock-on panel displays readable labels such as "Heal amount", "Heal cooldown", and "Flee speed" while preserving the same values.
